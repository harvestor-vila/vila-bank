part 1: the question stem of the original item
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate to compare the GDP Per Capita across the different cities?

part 2: options of the original item
(A) A pie chart with slices representing the proportion of GDP Per Capita for each city.
(B) A bar chart with cities on the x-axis and GDP Per Capita values on the y-axis.
(C) A 3D scatter plot with Population, GDP Per Capita, and Median Age on the three axes.
(D) A heatmap with cities on one axis and Population on the other, with colors representing GDP Per Capita.

part 3: the correct answer of the original item
(B) A bar chart with cities on the x-axis and GDP Per Capita values on the y-axis.
