part 1: the question stem of the original item
    The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate for comparing the relative proportions of different energy sources?

    part 2: options of the original item
    (A) A scatter plot with energy sources on the x-axis and percentages on the y-axis.
    (B) A pie chart with different slices representing the percentages of each energy source.
    (C) A line graph with time on the x-axis and energy source usage on the y-axis.
    (D) A bar chart with countries on the x-axis and energy source dependency on the y-axis.

    part 3: the correct answer of the original item
    (B) A pie chart with different slices representing the percentages of each energy source.
