part 1: the question stem of the original item
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate for comparing the Health Index values across all states?

part 2: options of the original item
(A) A pie chart with slices representing the Health Index values of each state.
(B) A choropleth map with states colored according to their Health Index values.
(C) A line graph with time on the x-axis and Health Index values on the y-axis.
(D) A scatter plot with states on the x-axis and Health Index values on the y-axis.

part 3: the correct answer of the original item
(B) A choropleth map with states colored according to their Health Index values.
