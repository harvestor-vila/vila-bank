part 1: the question stem of the original item
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate for comparing the relative energy consumption values across all U.S. states?

part 2: options of the original item
(A) A pie chart with each state represented as a slice proportional to its energy consumption value.
(B) A choropleth map of the United States with states colored according to their energy consumption values.
(C) A scatter plot with states on the x-axis and energy consumption values on the y-axis.
(D) A line graph with time on the x-axis and energy consumption values on the y-axis, with a line for each state.

part 3: the correct answer of the original item
(B) A choropleth map of the United States with states colored according to their energy consumption values.
