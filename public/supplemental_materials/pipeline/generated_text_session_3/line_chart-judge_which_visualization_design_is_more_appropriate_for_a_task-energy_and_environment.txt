part 1: the question stem of the original item
Considering the task of analyzing trends in energy consumption over time, which visualization design would be most appropriate? The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data.

part 2: options of the original item
(A) A pie chart showing the proportion of energy consumption for each time unit.
(B) A line chart with time on the x-axis and energy consumption on the y-axis, with points connected to show the trend over time.
(C) A bar chart with energy consumption categories on the x-axis and count of time units falling into each category on the y-axis.
(D) A scatter plot with time on the x-axis and energy consumption on the y-axis, without connecting lines or trend lines.

part 3: the correct answer of the original item
(B) A line chart with time on the x-axis and energy consumption on the y-axis, with points connected to show the trend over time.
