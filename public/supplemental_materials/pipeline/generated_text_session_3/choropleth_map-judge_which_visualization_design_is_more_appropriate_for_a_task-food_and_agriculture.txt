part 1: the question stem of the original item
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design is most appropriate for comparing the relative values of food and agriculture production across different states?

part 2: options of the original item
(A) A pie chart with different slices representing each state's production units.
(B) A choropleth map with varying colors representing the production units across states.
(C) A line graph with time on the x-axis and production units on the y-axis.
(D) A scatter plot with states on the x-axis and production units on the y-axis.

part 3: the correct answer of the original item
(B) A choropleth map with varying colors representing the production units across states.
