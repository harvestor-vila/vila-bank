part 1: the question stem of the original item
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate to display the trend of innovation scores over the years?

part 2: options of the original item
(A) A pie chart showing the proportion of each year's innovation score to the total sum of scores.
(B) A line chart with years on the x-axis and innovation scores on the y-axis, with points marking each year's score.
(C) A bar chart with different colors for each year, arranged alphabetically by year.
(D) A scatter plot with innovation scores on the x-axis and years on the y-axis, without connecting lines.

part 3: the correct answer of the original item
(B) A line chart with years on the x-axis and innovation scores on the y-axis, with points marking each year's score.
