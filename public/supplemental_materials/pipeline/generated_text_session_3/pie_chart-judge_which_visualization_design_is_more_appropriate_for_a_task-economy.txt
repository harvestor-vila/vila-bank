part 1: the question stem of the original item
    Which visualization design would be most appropriate for comparing the relative sizes of the different economic sectors represented in the pie chart? The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data.
    
    part 2: options of the original item
    (A) A scatter plot with sectors on the x-axis and percentages on the y-axis.
    (B) A bar chart with sectors on the x-axis and percentages on the y-axis.
    (C) A line graph with time on the x-axis and percentages on the y-axis, with lines representing different sectors.
    (D) A heatmap with sectors on one axis and percentages represented by color intensity.
    
    part 3: the correct answer of the original item
    (B) A bar chart with sectors on the x-axis and percentages on the y-axis.
