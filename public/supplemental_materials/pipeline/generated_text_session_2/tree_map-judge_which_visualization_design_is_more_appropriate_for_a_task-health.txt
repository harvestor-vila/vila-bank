part 1: the question stem
Considering the distribution of health subcategories by percentage, which visualization design would best facilitate the comparison of individual subcategory percentages within their respective categories? The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data.

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A pie chart with different colors representing each subcategory.
(B) A stacked bar chart with each category's subcategories stacked upon one another.
(C) A line graph with time on the x-axis and percentage on the y-axis, with lines representing each subcategory.
(D) A treemap with each category as a distinct color and subcategories labeled within the area proportional to their percentage.

part 3: the correct answer
(D) A treemap with each category as a distinct color and subcategories labeled within the area proportional to their percentage.
