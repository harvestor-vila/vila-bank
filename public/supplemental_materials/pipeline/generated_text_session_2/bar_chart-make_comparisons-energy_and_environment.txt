part 1: the question stem
Which energy source has a higher production level, Solar or Coal?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) Solar has a higher production level than Coal.
(B) Coal has a higher production level than Solar.
(C) Solar and Coal have the same production level.
(D) The production levels cannot be compared without specific values.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) Coal has a higher production level than Solar.
