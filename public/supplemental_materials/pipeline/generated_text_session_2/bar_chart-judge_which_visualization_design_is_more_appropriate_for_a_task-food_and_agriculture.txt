part 1: the question stem
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate for comparing the production values across different food items?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A pie chart with different colors representing each food item.
(B) A horizontal bar chart with bars representing production values for each food item.
(C) A scatter plot with food items on the x-axis and production values on the y-axis.
(D) A line graph with food items on the x-axis and production values plotted over time.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) A horizontal bar chart with bars representing production values for each food item.
