part 1: the question stem
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design is most appropriate for comparing the relative contributions of different sectors to the economy over multiple years?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A pie chart showing the percentage contribution of each sector for a single year.
(B) A 100 percent stacked bar chart showing the distribution of sector contributions over multiple years.
(C) A line graph showing the trend of one sector's contribution over multiple years.
(D) A scatter plot comparing two sectors' contributions against each other over multiple years.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) A 100 percent stacked bar chart showing the distribution of sector contributions over multiple years.
