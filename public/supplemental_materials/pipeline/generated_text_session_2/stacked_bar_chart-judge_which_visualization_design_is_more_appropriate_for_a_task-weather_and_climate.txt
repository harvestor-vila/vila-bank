part 1: the question stem
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate for comparing the total values of different climate factors across all cities?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A pie chart showing the proportion of each climate factor within each city.
(B) A stacked bar chart with cities on the x-axis and climate factor values stacked by color.
(C) A line graph with time on the x-axis and value on the y-axis, with separate lines for each climate factor.
(D) A scatter plot with climate factors on the x-axis and value on the y-axis, with different colors for each city.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) A stacked bar chart with cities on the x-axis and climate factor values stacked by color.
