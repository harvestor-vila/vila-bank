part 1: the question stem
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate for comparing the relative proportions of each category in the dataset?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A scatter plot with each category on the x-axis and the corresponding percentages on the y-axis.
(B) A pie chart with each slice representing a category and its size proportional to the percentage of that category.
(C) A line graph with time on the x-axis and the percentage of each category on the y-axis.
(D) A bar chart with categories on the y-axis and different colors representing different years on the x-axis.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) A pie chart with each slice representing a category and its size proportional to the percentage of that category.
