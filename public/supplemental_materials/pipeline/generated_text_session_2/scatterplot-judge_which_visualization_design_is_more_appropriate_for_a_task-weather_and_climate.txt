part 1: the question stem
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate to assess the strength and direction of the relationship between temperature and humidity?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A pie chart with slices representing different ranges of temperature and their corresponding average humidity levels.
(B) A scatterplot with temperature on the x-axis and humidity on the y-axis, possibly including a trend line to indicate the relationship.
(C) A bar chart with separate bars for temperature and humidity, sorted in ascending order of temperature.
(D) A stacked area chart showing the cumulative values of temperature and humidity over a sequence of data points.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) A scatterplot with temperature on the x-axis and humidity on the y-axis, possibly including a trend line to indicate the relationship.
