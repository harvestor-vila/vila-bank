part 1: the question stem
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate to assess the central tendency and dispersion of the cholesterol levels?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A pie chart showing the proportion of individuals within predefined cholesterol level ranges.
(B) A boxplot to visualize the distribution, median, and potential outliers of the cholesterol levels.
(C) A scatter plot with cholesterol levels on the y-axis and an arbitrary index on the x-axis.
(D) A line graph showing cholesterol levels trend over time.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) A boxplot to visualize the distribution, median, and potential outliers of the cholesterol levels.
