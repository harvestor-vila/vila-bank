part 1: the question stem
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design is most appropriate for showing the cumulative number of patients recovered over a period of time?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A pie chart showing the proportion of patients recovered each day out of the total recovered.
(B) An area chart with the days on the x-axis and the cumulative number of patients recovered on the y-axis.
(C) A scatter plot with individual days on the x-axis and the number of patients recovered that day on the y-axis.
(D) A bar chart with days on the x-axis and the difference in the number of patients recovered from the previous day on the y-axis.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) An area chart with the days on the x-axis and the cumulative number of patients recovered on the y-axis.
