part 1: the question stem
Which visualization design is most appropriate for showing the distribution of votes among political parties over time? The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data.

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A pie chart with different colors representing each political party and slices showing the proportion of votes for each year.
(B) A stacked area chart with time on the horizontal axis, cumulative votes on the vertical axis, and different colors representing each political party.
(C) A single bar chart with bars representing the total votes for all parties for each year.
(D) A scatter plot with individual years on the x-axis and vote counts on the y-axis, colored by political party.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) A stacked area chart with time on the horizontal axis, cumulative votes on the vertical axis, and different colors representing each political party.
