part 1: the question stem
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate for identifying trends in daily average temperatures over a period of 20 days?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A pie chart showing the proportion of days within certain temperature ranges.
(B) A line chart with days on the x-axis and temperature on the y-axis, with points marking each day's average temperature.
(C) A bar chart with temperature ranges on the x-axis and the number of days falling into each range on the y-axis.
(D) A scatter plot with days of the week on the x-axis and temperature on the y-axis, without connecting lines.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) A line chart with days on the x-axis and temperature on the y-axis, with points marking each day's average temperature.
