part 1: the question stem
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate for analyzing the trend of votes over time?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A pie chart showing the proportion of votes for each year.
(B) An area chart with the years on the x-axis and the number of votes on the y-axis.
(C) A scatter plot with each year's votes represented by a differently colored dot.
(D) A bar chart with different bars for each political party's votes each year.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) An area chart with the years on the x-axis and the number of votes on the y-axis.
