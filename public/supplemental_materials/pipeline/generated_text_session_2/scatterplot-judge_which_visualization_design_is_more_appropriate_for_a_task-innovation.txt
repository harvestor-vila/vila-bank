part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A pie chart with slices representing different ranges of Innovation Scores.
(B) A scatterplot with Research Investment on the x-axis and Innovation Scores on the y-axis, points representing data entries.
(C) A bar chart with bars representing total Research Investment for grouped ranges of Innovation Scores.
