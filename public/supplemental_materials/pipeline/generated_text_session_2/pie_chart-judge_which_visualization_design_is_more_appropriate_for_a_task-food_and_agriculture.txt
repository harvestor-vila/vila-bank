part 1: the question stem
Given the task of understanding the distribution of different food and agriculture sectors, judge which visualization design is more appropriate. The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data.

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A scatter plot with 'Category' on the x-axis and 'Percentage' on the y-axis, different colors for each category.
(B) A pie chart with sectors as slices proportional to their 'Percentage' values and labeled with 'Category' names.
(C) A line graph with 'Category' on the x-axis and 'Percentage' on the y-axis, connected by lines in the order of the categories.
(D) A bar chart with 'Category' on the x-axis and 'Percentage' on the y-axis, each bar representing a different sector.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) A pie chart with sectors as slices proportional to their 'Percentage' values and labeled with 'Category' names.
