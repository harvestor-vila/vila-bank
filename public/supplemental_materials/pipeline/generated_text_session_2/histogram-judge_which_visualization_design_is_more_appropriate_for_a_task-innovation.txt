part 1: the question stem
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate for understanding the central tendency and dispersion of the innovation scores?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A pie chart showing the proportion of scores in predefined ranges.
(B) A scatter plot with innovation scores on the x-axis and a constant value on the y-axis.
(C) A boxplot summarizing the median, quartiles, and outliers of the innovation scores.
(D) A line graph showing the trend of innovation scores over time.

part 3: the correct answer
(C) A boxplot summarizing the median, quartiles, and outliers of the innovation scores.
