part 1: the question stem of the original item
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate for comparing the average temperatures across all cities?

part 2: options of the original item
(A) A pie chart with slices representing the proportion of total temperature for each city.
(B) A bar chart with cities on the x-axis and average temperatures on the y-axis.
(C) A line graph with cities on the x-axis and average temperatures on the y-axis, connected by lines.
(D) A scatter plot with cities on the x-axis and average temperatures on the y-axis, represented by dots.

part 3: the correct answer of the original item
(B) A bar chart with cities on the x-axis and average temperatures on the y-axis.
