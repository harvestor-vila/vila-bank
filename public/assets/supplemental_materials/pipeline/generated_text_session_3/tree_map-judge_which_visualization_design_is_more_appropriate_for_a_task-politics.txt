part 1: the question stem of the original item
    The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate for comparing the relative sizes of the policy areas and their respective subcategories?

    part 2: options of the original item
    (A) A line chart with policy areas on the x-axis and percentage values on the y-axis.
    (B) A treemap with each policy area and subcategory represented as rectangles sized proportionally to their percentage values.
    (C) A scatter plot with policy areas on the x-axis and percentage values on the y-axis.
    (D) A pie chart with separate slices for each subcategory without distinguishing between policy areas.

    part 3: the correct answer of the original item
    (B) A treemap with each policy area and subcategory represented as rectangles sized proportionally to their percentage values.
