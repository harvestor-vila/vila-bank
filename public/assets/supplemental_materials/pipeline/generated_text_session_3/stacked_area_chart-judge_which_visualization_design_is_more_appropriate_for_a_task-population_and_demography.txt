part 1: The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design is most appropriate to display the change in population distribution across different age groups over time?

part 2: options
(A) A pie chart showing the proportion of each age group for a single year.
(B) A stacked area chart showing the population distribution across age groups over time.
(C) A scatter plot with individual years on the x-axis and population sizes on the y-axis, without distinguishing between age groups.
(D) A series of bar charts for each year, with separate bars for each age group.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) A stacked area chart showing the population distribution across age groups over time.
