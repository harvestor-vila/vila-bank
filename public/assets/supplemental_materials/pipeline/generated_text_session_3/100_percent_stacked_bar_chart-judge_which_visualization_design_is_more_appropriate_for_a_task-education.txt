part 1: the question stem of the original item
Which visualization design is most appropriate to compare the proportional distribution of subjects within each school? The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data.

part 2: options of the original item
(A) A pie chart for each school showing the distribution of subjects.
(B) A line graph with time on the x-axis and percentage of each subject on the y-axis.
(C) A scatter plot with schools on the x-axis and percentage of each subject on the y-axis.
(D) A 100 percent stacked bar chart with schools on the x-axis and the percentage of each subject represented in stacked segments.

part 3: the correct answer of the original item
(D) A 100 percent stacked bar chart with schools on the x-axis and the percentage of each subject represented in stacked segments.
