part 1: the question stem of the original item
Which visualization design would be most appropriate for comparing the relative profitability of different crops, taking into account their production and yield? The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data.

part 2: options of the original item
(A) A pie chart with slices representing the profit of each crop.
(B) A bubble chart with crop production on the x-axis, yield on the y-axis, and bubble size representing profit.
(C) A line graph showing the change in profit over time for each crop.
(D) A bar chart with bars representing the average temperature suitable for each crop's growth.

part 3: the correct answer of the original item
(B) A bubble chart with crop production on the x-axis, yield on the y-axis, and bubble size representing profit.
