part 1: the question stem of the original item
Given the distribution of agricultural yield per acre as shown, which visualization design would best allow a viewer to compare the median yield to the mean yield? The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data.

part 2: options of the original item
(A) A pie chart with slices representing the proportion of total yield per range of acres.
(B) A scatter plot with each point representing the yield per acre for individual data points.
(C) A box plot showing the median, quartiles, and potential outliers in the yield per acre.
(D) A line graph showing the trend of yield per acre over a sequential time period.

part 3: the correct answer of the original item
(C) A box plot showing the median, quartiles, and potential outliers in the yield per acre.
