part 1: the question stem
Considering the educational levels and their respective percentages, judge which visualization design is more appropriate for displaying the proportional distribution of a categorical dataset. The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data.

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A scatter plot with education levels on the x-axis and percentages on the y-axis.
(B) A pie chart with different segments representing the proportion of each education level.
(C) A line graph with education levels on the x-axis and percentages on the y-axis, connected by a line.
(D) A bar chart with education levels on the x-axis and different bar heights representing the frequency of each level.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) A pie chart with different segments representing the proportion of each education level.
