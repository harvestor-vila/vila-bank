part 1: the question stem
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate for comparing the relative sizes of the categories within the population?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A scatter plot with each category represented by a point whose position on the y-axis reflects its percentage.
(B) A pie chart with each slice representing a category and its size proportional to the percentage of the population it represents.
(C) A line graph with time on the x-axis and the percentage of each category on the y-axis.
(D) A bar chart with categories on the x-axis and different colors representing different years on the y-axis.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) A pie chart with each slice representing a category and its size proportional to the percentage of the population it represents.
