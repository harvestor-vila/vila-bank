The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate for comparing the relative proportions of political party preferences?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A line graph with time on the x-axis and percentage on the y-axis.
(B) A pie chart with different colors representing each political party and their respective percentages.
(C) A scatter plot with political parties on the x-axis and percentage on the y-axis.
(D) A bar chart with political parties on the x-axis and percentage on the y-axis, without any color distinction.

part 3: the correct answer
