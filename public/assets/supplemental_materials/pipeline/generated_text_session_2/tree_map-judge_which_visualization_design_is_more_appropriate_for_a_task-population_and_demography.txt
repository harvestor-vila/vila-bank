part 1: the question stem
Considering the dataset's categorical nature with hierarchical relationships and proportions, judge which visualization design is more appropriate for displaying this information. The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data.

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A pie chart with separate sections for each subcategory, without clear distinctions between different categories.
(B) A stacked bar chart with each bar representing a category and segments within the bars representing subcategories.
(C) A line graph with time on the x-axis and percentage on the y-axis, with different lines for each subcategory.
(D) A treemap with areas proportional to the percentage values, grouped by category and labeled by subcategory.

part 3: the correct answer
(D) A treemap with areas proportional to the percentage values, grouped by category and labeled by subcategory.
