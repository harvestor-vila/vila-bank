part 1: the question stem
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate to display the trend in energy consumption over the years?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A pie chart showing the proportion of energy consumption for each year.
(B) An area chart with the years on the x-axis and energy consumption on the y-axis, shaded beneath the line.
(C) A scatter plot with random x and y values not corresponding to years or energy consumption.
(D) A bar chart with different colors for each year, not ordered chronologically.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) An area chart with the years on the x-axis and energy consumption on the y-axis, shaded beneath the line.
