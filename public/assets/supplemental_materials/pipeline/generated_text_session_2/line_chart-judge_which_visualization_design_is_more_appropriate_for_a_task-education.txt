part 1: the question stem
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate to display the trend of student enrollment over the academic years from 2001 to 2020?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A pie chart with slices representing the proportion of enrollments for each academic year.
(B) A bar chart with vertical bars representing the number of students enrolled each academic year.
(C) A line chart with a point for each academic year connected by lines to show changes in enrollment over time.
(D) A scatter plot with each academic year's enrollment represented as an unconnected point on the plot.

part 3: the correct answer
(C) A line chart with a point for each academic year connected by lines to show changes in enrollment over time.
