part 1: the question stem
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate for understanding the distribution of standardized test scores?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A pie chart showing the proportion of students achieving each unique test score.
(B) A scatter plot with test scores on the x-axis and student count on the y-axis.
(C) A histogram with appropriate bin widths to show the frequency distribution of test scores.
(D) A line graph showing the trend of test scores over time.

part 3: the correct answer
(C) A histogram with appropriate bin widths to show the frequency distribution of test scores.
