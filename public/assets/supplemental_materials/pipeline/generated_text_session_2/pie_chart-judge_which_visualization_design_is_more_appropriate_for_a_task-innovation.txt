part 1: the question stem
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate for comparing the relative proportions of each innovation category?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A scatter plot with innovation categories on the x-axis and percentages on the y-axis.
(B) A pie chart with different slices representing the percentages of each innovation category.
(C) A line graph with time on the x-axis and percentages on the y-axis, with different lines for each category.
(D) A bar chart with countries on the x-axis and GDP on the y-axis.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) A pie chart with different slices representing the percentages of each innovation category.
