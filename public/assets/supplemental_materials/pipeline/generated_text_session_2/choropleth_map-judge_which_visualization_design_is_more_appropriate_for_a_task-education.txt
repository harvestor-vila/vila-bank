part 1: the question stem
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate for comparing the education values across all states to identify regional patterns and outliers?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A pie chart with slices representing the education values for each state.
(B) A choropleth map with states colored according to their education values.
(C) A line graph with time on the x-axis and education values on the y-axis.
(D) A bar chart with each state's name on the x-axis and their corresponding education values on the y-axis.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) A choropleth map with states colored according to their education values.
