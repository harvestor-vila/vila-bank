part 1: the question stem
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would best allow a viewer to compare the total amount of precipitation with the average temperature over the 20-day period?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A pie chart showing the proportion of days with different temperature ranges.
(B) A line chart with two y-axes, one for precipitation and one for average temperature over time.
(C) A scatter plot with precipitation on the x-axis and temperature on the y-axis.
(D) A bar chart with categories for different wind speeds.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) A line chart with two y-axes, one for precipitation and one for average temperature over time.
