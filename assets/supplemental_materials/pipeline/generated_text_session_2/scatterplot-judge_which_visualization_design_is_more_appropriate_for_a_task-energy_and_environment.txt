part 1: the question stem
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate to understand the relationship between Energy Consumption and Carbon Emission?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A pie chart showing the proportion of Energy Consumption categories to the total.
(B) A scatterplot with Energy Consumption on the x-axis and Carbon Emission on the y-axis, with points representing data entries.
(C) A bar chart with Energy Consumption on the x-axis and the count of entries in each Energy Consumption category on the y-axis.
(D) A line graph showing the trend of Carbon Emission over time.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) A scatterplot with Energy Consumption on the x-axis and Carbon Emission on the y-axis, with points representing data entries.
