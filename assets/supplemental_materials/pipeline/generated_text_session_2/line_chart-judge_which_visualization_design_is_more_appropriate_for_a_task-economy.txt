part 1: the question stem
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate to display the trend in annual GDP growth rates over the years?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A pie chart showing the proportion of each year's GDP growth rate to the total sum of growth rates.
(B) A line chart with years on the x-axis and GDP growth rates on the y-axis, with points marking each year's growth rate.
(C) A bar chart with different colors representing each year's GDP growth rate.
(D) A scatter plot with GDP growth rates on the x-axis and years on the y-axis.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) A line chart with years on the x-axis and GDP growth rates on the y-axis, with points marking each year's growth rate.
