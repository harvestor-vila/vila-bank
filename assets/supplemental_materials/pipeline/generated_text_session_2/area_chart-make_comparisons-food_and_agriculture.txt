part 1: the question stem
How does the agricultural production in 2000 compare to the production in 2019?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) The production in 2000 was higher than in 2019.
(B) The production in 2000 was the same as in 2019.
(C) The production in 2000 was lower than in 2019.
(D) The production in 2000 and 2019 cannot be compared.

part 3: the correct answer
(C) The production in 2000 was lower than in 2019.
