part 1: the question stem
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate for comparing the total energy production from all sources for each country?

part 2: the option set that includes the correct answer and three incorrect options
(A) A pie chart showing the percentage contribution of each energy source to the total global energy production.
(B) A stacked bar chart with countries on the x-axis, total production on the y-axis, and different colors representing different energy sources.
(C) A line graph with time on the x-axis and energy production on the y-axis, with separate lines for each country.
(D) A scatter plot with energy production on the x-axis and countries on the y-axis, with points colored by energy source.

part 3: the correct answer
(B) A stacked bar chart with countries on the x-axis, total production on the y-axis, and different colors representing different energy sources.
