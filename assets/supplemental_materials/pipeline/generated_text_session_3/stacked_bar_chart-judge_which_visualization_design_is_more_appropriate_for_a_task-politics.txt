part 1: the question stem of the original item
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would best allow a viewer to compare the total number of votes for each issue across all states?

part 2: options of the original item
(A) A pie chart with different slices representing each issue's total votes across all states.
(B) A stacked bar chart with states on the x-axis, total votes on the y-axis, and issues represented by different colors.
(C) A line graph with states on the x-axis, total votes on the y-axis, and separate lines for each issue.
(D) A scatter plot with each state-issue pair as a point, positioned by the number of votes on the x-axis and the state on the y-axis.

part 3: the correct answer of the original item
(B) A stacked bar chart with states on the x-axis, total votes on the y-axis, and issues represented by different colors.
