part 1: the question stem of the original item
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate for comparing the total number of students across all grades for each school?

part 2: options of the original item
(A) A pie chart with different slices representing each school and grade combination.
(B) A stacked bar chart with schools on the x-axis, number of students on the y-axis, and different colors representing each grade.
(C) A line graph with grades on the x-axis, number of students on the y-axis, and separate lines for each school.
(D) A scatter plot with schools on the x-axis and number of students on the y-axis, with no distinction between grades.

part 3: the correct answer of the original item
(B) A stacked bar chart with schools on the x-axis, number of students on the y-axis, and different colors representing each grade.
