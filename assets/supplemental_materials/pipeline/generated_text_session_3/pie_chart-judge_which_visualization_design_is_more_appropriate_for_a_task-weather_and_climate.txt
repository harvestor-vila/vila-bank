part 1: the question stem of the original item
The visualization above is only a demonstration of the relevant data. Which visualization design would be most appropriate for comparing the relative proportions of different climate categories?

part 2: options of the original item
(A) A scatter plot with climate categories on the x-axis and percentages on the y-axis.
(B) A pie chart with different climate categories represented as slices proportional to their percentage.
(C) A line graph with climate categories on the x-axis and percentages on the y-axis.
(D) A bar chart with climate categories on the x-axis and different colors representing each percentage.

part 3: the correct answer of the original item
(B) A pie chart with different climate categories represented as slices proportional to their percentage.
